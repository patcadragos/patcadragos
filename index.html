Follice Box

The FOLLICE BOX is an autonomous follow-me cooler designed to transport heavy loads in outdoor environments without manual effort. It combines a 50L insulated storage unit with a motorized chassis powered by a 24V system. Using a differential drive mechanism, the cooler can move smoothly, follow a user, reach speeds of up to 1.2 m/s, and climb slopes of 10°. The system offers a compact, stable, and cost-efficient solution for improving mobility and user comfort.

Autonomous Polishing Assembly

A real-life CNC project: an autonomous polishing machine built with Bosch profiles, a universal plastic box, and a rotating magnetic disc containing steel filings. Custom motor bracket, 8 magnets, and an electric control panel with emergency stop. All components manufactured on CNC from the design to final assembly.
